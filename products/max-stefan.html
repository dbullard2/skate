Nike SB Air Max Stefan Janoski 2 Premium

The Nike SB Air Max Stefan Janoski 2 hugs your foot with a breathable textile upper. A Max Air unit in the heel cushions every step, while a flat rubber outsole optimizes grip when you're on your board.

Features:

  • Textile upper delivers a breathable, sock-like fit.
  • Visible Max Air unit in the heel cushions each step.
  • Auxetic traction pattern helps disperse pressure.
  • VFull rubber outsole offers traction and durability. The outsole is flatter than the Nike SB Air Max Janoski to provide better grip, and more stability, when you skate.
  • The original 1-piece bootie has been redesigned for a closer, second-skin-like fit.
  • Midfoot overlay helps keep your feet secure.
  • Low cut for a natural fit and comfort around your ankles.
  • Full-length IP foam midsole provides superior comfort and cushioning.
  • 100% guaranteed authentic
