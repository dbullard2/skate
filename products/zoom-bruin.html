Nike SB Zoom Bruin Low

The Nike SB Zoom Bruin Low Women's Skateboarding Shoe isn’t a shrunk-down take of the men's version, it’s built for women from the ground up with a shallower toe and more tapered heel for better boardfeel. This is a feminine take on a time-honored shoe.

Features:

  • Design is tailored to women's feet for optimal fit, while still featuring classic Bruin design lines.
  • Sockliner features a Zoom Air unit in the heel to help cushion hard landings.
  • Suede upper has a soft, broken-in feel
  • Vulcanized construction delivers grip and traction
  • 100% guaranteed authentic
